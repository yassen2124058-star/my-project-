Solder-Meter System

Integration. Precision. Sustainability.

A revolutionary Grade 11 Capstone Project repurposing E-Waste into high-precision scientific tools.

See the Results

The Innovation

The Crisis

Egypt generates 690,000 Tonnes of E-waste annually. Valuable components are buried, releasing toxins into the soil.

The Solution

We salvage heating elements and meter casings to build a Hybrid Tool: A Soldering Iron + Multimeter in one device.

The Tech

Integrated Buck Converters for voltage regulation and a custom-wired selector dial for instant mode switching.
